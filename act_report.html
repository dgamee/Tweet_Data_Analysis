Title: Data wrangling report on WeRateDogs Twitter account tweets from 2015 to 2017¶

Introduction¶

The dataset contains data from the tweet archive of Twitter user @dog_rates, also known as WeRateDogs. WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. These ratings almost always have a denominator of 10. The numerators, though? Almost always greater than 10. WeRateDogs has over a million followers and has received international media coverage. During the course of analysis. The following insights were deduced from the analysis.

Insights Questions¶

  1. Most predicted Dog breed

  2. Tweet with the highest retweet

  3. Most frequent tweet source

Insight #1: Most predicted dog breed¶

From the result of the analysis, the most predicted dog breed was seen to be the Golden_retriever with a total prediction count of 137, followed by the labrador_retriever. The least most predicted according to the top 30 bar graph below, was west_higland_white_Terrier.
